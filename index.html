Dog-Faced Water Snake

Scientific name : Cerberus rynchops

Alternate names : New Guinea bockadam, South Asian bockadam, Bockadam snake

Order : Squamata

Family : Homalopsidae

IUCN Read List Category : Least Concern

Features : They are striped and dark brown in colouration. Duvernouy's glands present on both sides of head which are considered primitive venom glands which help with swallowing and are mildly venomous.

Biology : Ovovivparous, Lives in near-shore coastal waters including marine, brackish, and freshwater environments. Habitats include mostly mangrove forests, tidal mudflats, and estuarine areas.

Locality : Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh

Distribution : Coastal areas of India (Gujarat, Maharashtra, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, etc.), Bangladesh, Myanmar, Thailand, NW Malaysia, Indonesia (Borneo).
